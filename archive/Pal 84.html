Nature Of Business : R&D
Designation : Product Engineer
Tentative Job Location : Ahmedabad, Gujarat
Description :

Role:

  • Design of Joineries and Components in a Modular system.
  • Prototyping and Testing and In-depth analysis of designed components.
  • Documentation of the Design Ideas, Testing Results, and Conclusions.
  • Communication with Experts in relevant field.
  • Creation of Manuals and SOPs.
  • Training and Implementation of Components in field.

 

Requirements:

  • Good understanding of the Fundamentals of Strength of Materials.
  • Good understanding of the design and development process of any product.

  • Creative mind. The product design engineer is expected to generate a lot of ideas for any given problem statement.

 

Skills / Attitude:

  • Open-minded: Should always have a positive attitude towards the problem.
  • 3D Modelling: Any software.
  • Finite element analysis (FEA)
  • Strong analytical Skills
  • Strong hands-on skills and knowledge of Mechanical workshop tools.
  • Good Communication.
  • Problem-solving attitude.
  • Good documentation.
